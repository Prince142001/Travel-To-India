Indus River, Pakistan

Level of difficulty : Very basic ‐ Grade I Rapids

Distance Covered : A stretch of 25 kms is covered

Best time for rafting : early November to March end

Nothing can be more adventurous then river rafting in Indus river- known as 'Singhe Khababs' or 'Out of the Lion's Mouth'. Offering magnetising views of Ladakh and Zanskar Ranges, rafting in Indus makes for one unforgettable experience in India. Imagine taming the rapids while gulping down majestic views of mighty mountains all around!
