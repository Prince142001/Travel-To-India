Lohit River, India,China

Level of difficulty : Moderate‐ Grade IV rapid

Distance Covered : Total distance of approx 100 km from the Line of Actual Control to Hayuliang

Best time for rafting : September to June

If you're looking for a rafting adventure in the North East, Lohit river, flowing through Arunachal Pradesh is where you need to be. This lesser known river is one of the most interesting rivers for water sports like river rafting and kayaking in India. With fierce rapids and serene views, a river rafting feat in these waters won't disappoint!
